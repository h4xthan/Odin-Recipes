Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its rich, creamy sauce and comforting flavor. Freshly cooked fettuccine is coated in a smooth sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, creating a simple yet satisfying meal.

Although the recipe uses only a handful of ingredients, using freshly grated Parmesan and cooking the pasta to al dente makes a big difference. It pairs well with grilled chicken, shrimp, or a fresh green salad.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz (340g) fettuccine pasta
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
  2. Cook the fettuccine according to the packages instructions until al dente.
  3. While the pasta cooks, melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for about one minute if using.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and simmer for several minutes.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until the sauce becomes smooth.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the skillet.
  8. Toss until the noodles are fully coated in the sauce.
  9. Season with salt and black pepper.
  10. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
