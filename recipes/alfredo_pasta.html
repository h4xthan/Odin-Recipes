Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its rich, creamy sauce and comforting flavor. Freshly cooked fettuccine is coated in a smooth sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, creating a simple yet satisfying meal.

Although the recipe uses only a handful of ingredients, using freshly grated Parmesan and cooking the pasta to al dente makes a big difference. It pairs well with grilled chicken, shrimp, or a fresh green salad. Home
