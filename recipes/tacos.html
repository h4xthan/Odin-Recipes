Carne asada tacos

Description

Carne Asada Tacos are a popular Mexican street food featuring grilled, marinated steak served on warm corn tortillas. They are simple, fresh, and full of smoky flavor.

Fresh cilantro, diced onion, and a squeeze of lime complement the juicy steak perfectly, making these tacos ideal for lunch, dinner, or outdoor cookouts.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds flank or skirt steak
  • Olive oil
  • Lime juice
  • Orange juice
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Ground cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Corn tortillas
  • Diced onion
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Combine the marinade ingredients.
  2. Marinate the steak for at least two hours.
  3. Grill the steak over high heat until cooked to your preferred doneness.
  4. Let the meat rest for several minutes.
  5. Slice the steak into thin strips and chop into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Warm the tortillas.
  7. Fill each tortilla with steak.
  8. Top with onion and cilantro.
  9. Squeeze fresh lime over the tacos.
  10. Serve immediately.
