Shrimp cocktail

Description

Shrimp Cocktail is a refreshing seafood appetizer made with cooked shrimp served in a chilled tomato-based sauce. The sauce is tangy, slightly spicy, and full of fresh vegetables, making it especially popular during warm weather.

In Mexican cuisine, shrimp cocktail is often served in a tall glass with crackers or tostadas on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cooked shrimp
  • Tomato juice
  • Lime juice
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Jalapeño
  • Cilantro
  • Hot sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Avocado (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel and chill the cooked shrimp
  2. Mix the tomato juice, lime juice and hot sauce.
  3. Dice the vegetables.
  4. Add the vegetables to the sauce.
  5. Stir in the shrimp.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  8. Add avocado if desired.
  9. Serve chilled.
  10. Enjoy with tostadas.
